We collect account information (username, email) and TikTok account credentials through OAuth 2.0 authentication.

With your consent, we access basic profile information, video analytics, and content you authorize us to post through TikTok's official API.

We collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data for service improvement purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use TikTok's official API to post content on your behalf. We only access TikTok data you explicitly authorize. We comply with TikTok's API Terms of Service. You can revoke access at any time through TikTok settings.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. Data is stored securely and encrypted where appropriate. We do not store your TikTok password (authentication via OAuth).

You have the right to access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, delete your data, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.

8. Cookies and Tracking

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.
